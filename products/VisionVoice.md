## What it does
- **Speak** — multi-language TTS (English, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, French, Hindi, Arabic, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian — male + female), picked/blended from a local catalog.
- **Listen** — wake-word activation + dictation + a full voice control loop (talk to your agent, it talks back).
- **Speaker lock (security)** — optional speaker verification so the voice loop **only obeys the enrolled owner's voice** (fail-closed when locked + unverifiable).
- **Route** — voice in → the same role/team dispatch that powers the rest of the VisionPRIME mind.

## Why it matters
Voice is what turns a tool into a presence. It's the difference between *running a command* and *working with a partner* — and the speaker lock keeps that partner yours alone.

## Setup (you control the credentials)
On first run, VisionVoice **prompts you for any voice-provider credentials** you choose to use (your own keys, local vault). The default TTS/STT path runs **locally** — no cloud account required to start.
