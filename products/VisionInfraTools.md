## The problem

An ungoverned coding agent will happily merge to main, push a deploy, or make a repo public with no guardrails — and it has none of the operational instincts an experienced team relies on for security triage, deploy readiness, or recovering from harness drift. You want the throughput of an agent without the blast radius.

## The solution

VisionInfraTools is a bundle of four production engineering skills that give an AI coding agent senior-grade infra judgment — with **autonomy on a seatbelt**. Each skill does the work right up to any dangerous, irreversible step, then prepares it (a PR, a draft, a plan) and asks for permission. The knowledge lives in your skills directory as pure Markdown — no service to run, no SaaS dependency, no telemetry, nothing to keep patched — and activates automatically on the matching task.

## What you get

- **vision-secops.** Triages Dependabot/CVE alerts by severity, opens dependency-fix branches + PRs, scans for hardcoded secrets, and audits ingress/vault exposure and compliance drift. Merging security PRs and applying firewall/DNS/access changes stay gated.
- **vision-devops.** Audits deploy readiness, CI/pipeline status, manifest health, and cost growth, and proposes + builds automations — without pushing anything to production. Applying to prod and pushing-to-main are gated.
- **vision-github-admin.** Opens and triages issues and PRs, manages labels, drafts release notes, and audits branch protection and security-alert hygiene. Merging to main, making a repo public, and changing org settings are gated.
- **vision-self-heal.** Detects drift in hooks, rules, governance, and vault state across upgrades, then non-destructively restores the control files from your committed source, re-seals the vault, and re-wires missing hooks.
- **Activation by intent.** Skills carry trigger descriptions, so the agent pulls in SecOps on a CVE mention, DevOps on a deploy, GitHub-admin on issues/PRs/releases, and self-heal on config drift — no manual prompting.
- **Learns from you.** Every skill logs its outcomes (action, result, your corrections) so it sharpens over time.

## Why trust it

- **Gated by design** — merge-to-main, deploy/push-to-main, making a repo public, and firewall/DNS changes are never auto-executed; they're drafted and held for your explicit approval.
- **Zero runtime footprint** — pure Markdown skills: no build step, no dependencies to pin, no telemetry, nothing to keep patched.
- **Findings surfaced, not buried** — each skill routes findings to a durable queue and sends time-sensitive items as notifications; anything that must go out is drafted, never auto-sent.

## Who it's for

Platform and DevOps teams who want their agent to operate at a senior engineer's standard across security, deploy, and repo administration — with a hard line between autonomous work and gated actions.
